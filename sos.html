Stay prepared
Safety tips for crowded temples
Carry identification at all times
Keep a government ID and a card with your emergency contact's number on your person, especially in large crowds.
Agree on a meeting point
Before entering a crowded temple, set a clear meeting point with your group in case anyone gets separated.
Keep your phone charged
SOS and live location sharing rely on your phone — carry a power bank for long pilgrimage days.
Stay hydrated
Long queues in heat can cause dehydration — water stations are marked on the temple's facilities map.

Frequently asked questions
What happens after I press SOS?

Your live location is shared instantly with temple security and the nearest medical post. A response team is typically dispatched within 4 minutes.

How do I claim a found item?

Click "Report item" and select "I lost an item," describing it in detail. Our team cross-checks it against the found-items log and verifies ownership before releasing it.

Is the SOS button monitored at all times?

Yes — the SOS system is monitored 24/7 by both DivyaYatra's safety team and on-ground temple security staff.
