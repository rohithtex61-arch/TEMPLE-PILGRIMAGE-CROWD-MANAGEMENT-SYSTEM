Travel smart
Planning tips for multi-temple trips
Travel during off-peak seasons
Avoid major festival dates unless that's specifically your goal — crowds can be 3-5x higher during festivals like Diwali or Maha Shivaratri.
Book darshan slots before departure
Reserve slots for each temple on your route at least 3 days in advance to guarantee priority entry.
Pre-book accommodation near temples
Many temple towns have limited lodging — book at least a week ahead for weekend or festival travel.
Carry essentials for long queues
Water, comfortable footwear, and any medication — some temple queues involve hours of standing.

Frequently asked questions
Can I customize a curated circuit?

Yes — select "Use this route" and you can add, remove, or reorder temple stops before saving it as your active itinerary.

Does the planner book darshan slots automatically?

Not automatically — you'll need to confirm darshan slots for each temple individually from the Booking page, but the planner highlights when to book.

Are travel times accurate?

Estimated drive times are based on typical road conditions and may vary with weather, traffic, or seasonal road closures in hill regions.
