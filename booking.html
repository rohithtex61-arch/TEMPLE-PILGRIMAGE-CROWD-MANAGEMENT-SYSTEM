Simple process
How darshan booking works
1
Choose temple & time
Pick from any of our 1,240+ listed temples and select an available time slot for the date you plan to visit.
2
Enter pilgrim details
Provide your name, mobile number, and the number of pilgrims in your group — no payment required.
3
Receive your QR pass
A QR-coded entry pass is generated instantly and stored on your device, even without an internet connection.
4
Scan & enter
Show your QR pass at the priority gate. Average entry time after booking is under 10 minutes.
Good to know
Booking policies
Free Rescheduling
Change your slot up to 12 hours before your booking at no charge, subject to availability.
No-show Policy
Unused slots are released automatically 15 minutes after the booked time to keep queues moving.
Priority Access
Senior citizens (60+) and differently-abled pilgrims get dedicated low-wait lanes at every temple.
Group Bookings
Book for up to 10 pilgrims in a single reservation; larger groups should contact temple administration directly.
ID Requirements
Carry a valid government ID matching the name on your booking for verification at the gate.
Always Free
DivyaYatra never charges a booking fee or commission — the platform is fully free for pilgrims.
Questions
Frequently asked questions
Is there really no booking fee?

Correct — DivyaYatra does not charge pilgrims any booking fee or commission. The platform is funded through partnerships with temple trusts, not pilgrim charges.

Can I book for someone else?

Yes, simply enter their name and the number of pilgrims in your party — the QR pass will reflect the names provided at booking.

What happens if I miss my slot?

Your reserved slot is held for 15 minutes past the booked time. After that, you'll need to book a new slot subject to same-day availability.

Do I need internet access to use my QR pass?

No — once generated, your QR pass is stored locally on your device and works offline at the gate scanner.
