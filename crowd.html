Behind the scenes
How live crowd data is generated
1
Entry-gate sensors & ticketing counts
Each gate reports footfall counts at fixed intervals, combined with QR-pass scan-ins from DivyaYatra bookings.
2
Capacity modeling
Live counts are compared against each temple's safe-capacity threshold to calculate the percentage shown.
3
Wait-time estimation
Historical throughput rates per gate are used to estimate current wait times, updated every 2 minutes.
4
Alerts & recommendations
When capacity crosses 80%, pilgrims and temple admins automatically receive alerts with alternate gate suggestions.
Questions
Frequently asked questions
How accurate is the live crowd data?

Data is refreshed every 2 minutes from gate sensors and ticketing systems, giving an accuracy of roughly ±5% during normal operations.

Can I get notified when crowd levels drop?

Yes — enable Crowd Alerts from your profile settings to receive a push notification when your chosen temple drops below 50% capacity.

Why are some gates "restricted" during peak hours?

Temple authorities may temporarily close secondary gates during extreme peak hours to better manage flow through the main entry and ensure pilgrim safety.

Does crowd data affect my existing booking?

No. If you have a confirmed darshan slot, you are guaranteed priority entry through Gate 2 regardless of general crowd levels.
